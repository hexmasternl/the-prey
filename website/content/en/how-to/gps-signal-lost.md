---
title: "GPS Signal Lost"
description: "What to do when your GPS signal drops in The Prey — restore background location, move to open sky, and get your position transmitting again."
keywords: ["GPS signal lost", "location not updating", "background location", "GPS troubleshooting", "The Prey help"]
---

<ol class="steps">
<li>Check your device's status bar. A GPS icon with a slash or no icon at all means location services are off or restricted.</li>
<li>Open your device <strong>Settings → Location</strong> and confirm location is enabled system-wide.</li>
<li>Open <strong>Settings → Apps → The Prey → Permissions → Location</strong> and confirm it is set to <strong>Always Allow</strong>.</li>
<li>If the app is in the background, bring it to the foreground. Some devices suspend background GPS when battery saver is active.</li>
<li>Disable <strong>Battery Saver</strong> mode during gameplay. It restricts background app activity and can cut GPS transmissions.</li>
<li>If signal is still lost, force-close the app and reopen it. The GPS service restarts on launch.</li>
<li>Inform the Game Creator if your signal was lost for an extended period. Decisions about whether that time period counts are at the Game Creator's discretion.</li>
<li>On Android: if the background service notification disappears from your notification shade, the foreground service has stopped. Reopen the app to restart it.</li>
</ol>
