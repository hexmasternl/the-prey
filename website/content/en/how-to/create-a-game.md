---
title: "Create a Game"
description: "Set up a session, assign roles, and start the hunt."
---

<ol class="steps">
<li>Open The Prey app and tap <strong>Create Game</strong>.</li>
<li>Select a saved playfield or draw a new one. The playfield defines the game boundary.</li>
<li>The app generates a join code. Share it with all participants before proceeding.</li>
<li>Wait for players to join. They appear in the lobby list as they connect.</li>
<li>Assign each player a role — <strong>Prey</strong> or <strong>Hunter</strong>. Every game needs at least one of each.</li>
<li>Assign yourself a role if you are playing.</li>
<li>Confirm all players are assigned and ready.</li>
<li>Tap <strong>Start Game</strong>. The 10-minute head start begins. Prey scatter. Hunters wait.</li>
</ol>
