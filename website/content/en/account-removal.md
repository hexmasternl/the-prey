_Last updated: 16 June 2026_

You can ask us to remove your The Prey account and the personal data associated with it at any time.

---

## How to Request Account Removal

To request the removal of your account, send an email to **account-removals@theprey.nl**.

Please send the request from the email address linked to your account, or include enough detail for us to identify your account, so we can verify the request and process it correctly.

---

## What Happens Next

Once we receive and verify your request, we will remove or anonymise your personal data, except where we are legally required to retain it. This includes your account information and the game data associated with your account.

---

## Contact

Questions about account removal? Email us at **account-removals@theprey.nl**.
