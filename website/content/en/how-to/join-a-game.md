---
title: "Join a Game"
description: "How to join a game in The Prey — enter the join code, land in the lobby, and get assigned your role by the Game Creator."
keywords: ["join a game", "join code", "lobby", "get a role", "The Prey how-to"]
---

<ol class="steps">
<li>Get the join code from the Game Creator. It is displayed in their app on the game lobby screen.</li>
<li>Open The Prey app on your device.</li>
<li>Tap <strong>Join Game</strong> on the home screen.</li>
<li>Enter the join code and tap <strong>Join</strong>.</li>
<li>You land in the lobby. Wait for the Game Creator to assign your role.</li>
<li>Grant location permissions when prompted. Select <strong>Always Allow</strong>.</li>
<li>Once the Game Creator launches the game, your role screen appears. The head start begins immediately.</li>
</ol>
