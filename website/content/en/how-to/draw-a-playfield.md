---
title: "Draw a Playfield"
description: "Define the arena — before the game starts."
---

<ol class="steps">
<li>Open The Prey app and navigate to <strong>Playfields</strong>.</li>
<li>Tap <strong>New Playfield</strong>. The map opens centered on your current GPS position.</li>
<li>Tap the map to place your first vertex. A pin appears.</li>
<li>Continue tapping to place each corner of your boundary polygon. Place at least three vertices.</li>
<li>Use <strong>pinch</strong> to zoom in and out for precision. Use <strong>drag</strong> on any vertex to reposition it.</li>
<li>To remove a vertex, tap it. To add one on an edge, long press the edge between two vertices.</li>
<li>When the shape looks correct, tap <strong>Confirm Polygon</strong>.</li>
<li>Enter a name for the playfield and tap <strong>Save</strong>.</li>
<li>The playfield is now available to select when creating a new game.</li>
</ol>
